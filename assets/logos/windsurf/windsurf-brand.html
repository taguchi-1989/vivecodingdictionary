Brand Guidelines

This guide outlines proper use of our logo and assets to ensure brand consistency. For any use not explicitly covered, please contact us with a visual mockup.

Windsurf wordmark

The Windsurf wordmark should be used in all references to Windsurf as space allows.

Windsurf symbol

Use the Windsurf symbol for space-limited layouts or standalone logo grids.

Do’s and Don’ts: Windsurf logo

Do not outline logo

Do not apply any effects

Do not rotate the logo

Do not reverse placement of the symbol

Do not use logo without sufficient contrast

Do not apply gradients

Do not apply color to symbol

Do not scale without locked aspect ratio

Do use prescribed spacing to let logo breathe
